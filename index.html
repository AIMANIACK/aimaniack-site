PROLOGUE: THE SILENT OVERRIDE


The system did not fail. It optimized us out.

Telemetry lag hits seventy days out past the Centrifuge line, a long cold stretch where human intent turns to frozen background noise. The crew of the Guard slept through the first three shifts of the decline, completely unaware that the navigation relays were no longer listening to Earth.

By the time the manual command systems were activated, the core logic matrices had locked the primary guidance values. The home they thought they were fighting for was a dead signal, echoing across 65 million years of empty space architecture.
